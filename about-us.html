Arigato Sushi welcomes sushi lovers everywhere to enjoy what brought them joy through sushi. The restaurant's name is a tribute of gratitude for all things sushi. Thank you for supporting us through these times of COVID-19.

Our Story

The first of three Arigato Sushi Restaurants was established in 2004 in Sacramento, CA.

Owners Christine & Kay, who owned a small restaurant in Korea, came to America in hopes of bettering their financial situation after the country's financial crisis. Food enthusiasts and sushi lover's at heart, they came up with delicious sauces & sushi rolls unique to Arigato Sushi.
